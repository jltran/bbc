Berkeley Builds Capacity

We’re Berkeley Builds Capacity! We are a group of volunteers and paid staff working to reduce HIV and the negative consequences associated with related substance use among East Bay college students. We’re a five year collaboration (2010-2015) between the Berkeley Free Clinic and UC Berkeley’s University Health Services funded by SAMHSA.
